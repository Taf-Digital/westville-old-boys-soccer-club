UNDER 13 DIVISION

The Functional Phase: Preparing for 11-v-11

Development Focus

This is the gateway to full-sized football. We focus on the physical and tactical demands of playing on a much larger pitch.

  • Endurance: Building the fitness required for full-pitch matches.
  • Formations: Deep dive into the 4-3-3 or 4-4-2 systems.
  • Set Pieces: Introducing basic corner and free-kick routines.

Training Info

Training Days:Mondays and Wednesdays

Time: 18:20 – 19:20

Location: Jimmy Bellows Sports Field

Focus: Confidence and fun on the ball.
