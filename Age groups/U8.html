UNDER 8 DIVISION

The Discovery Phase: Basic Coordination

Development Focus

We begin to bridge the gap between individual play and team awareness. We focus on coordination and the very basics of passing to a teammate.

  • Two-Footedness: Encouraging the use of both left and right feet.
  • Passing Basics: Learning how to use the inside of the foot for accuracy.
  • Game Logic: Understanding which direction to attack and defend.

Training Info

Training Days:Mondays and Wednesdays

Time: 17:15 – 18:15

Location: Jimmy Bellows Sports Field

Focus: Confidence and fun on the ball.
