UNDER 7 DIVISION

The Discovery Phase: Freedom to Play

Development Focus

At U7, we encourage creativity and dribbling. We want players to be brave on the ball and try new things without fear of making mistakes.

  • Creative Dribbling: Encouraging players to take on opponents 1-v-1.
  • Spatial Awareness: Learning to stay inside the pitch boundaries.
  • Social Skills: Introducing basic sportsmanship and listening to coaches.

Training Info

Training Days:Mondays and Wednesdays

Time: 17:15 – 18:15

Location: Jimmy Bellows Sports Field

Focus: Confidence and fun on the ball.
