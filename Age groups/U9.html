UNDER 9 DIVISION

The Discovery Phase: The Entry to Teamwork

Development Focus

The final step of Small Sided Games. We move from "me and the ball" to "us and the ball," introducing basic team shapes like triangles.

  • Support Play: Learning to move into open space to receive a pass.
  • Defending: Basic "staying between the ball and the goal" concepts.
  • Transition: Reacting quickly when the ball is lost or won.

Training Info

Training Days:Mondays and Wednesdays

Time: 17:15 – 18:15

Location: Jimmy Bellows Sports Field

Focus: Confidence and fun on the ball.
