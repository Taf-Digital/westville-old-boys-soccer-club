SENIOR FOOTBALL

The Excellence Phase: Club Pride & Performance

Performance Focus

Our Senior program represents the final stage of the Westville Old Boys pathway. We focus on high-intensity match preparation and maintaining the club's winning culture.

  • Match Tactics: Implementing sophisticated game plans to exploit opponent weaknesses and control the tempo.
  • Physical Peak: Sustaining elite match fitness and explosive power required for 90-minute competition.
  • Club Mentorship: Seniors serve as the standard-bearers, inspiring and leading the younger generations of the club.

Training Info

Training Days:Mondays and Wednesdays

Time: 18:20 – 19:20

Location: Jimmy Bellows Sports Field

Focus: Confidence and fun on the ball.
