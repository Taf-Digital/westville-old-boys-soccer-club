U11 DIVISION

The Functional Phase: Technical Precision

Development Focus

We focus on high-speed technique. Players are challenged to execute skills faster and under more pressure from opponents.

  • Scanning: Looking over the shoulder before receiving the ball.
  • Combination Play: One-twos and overlapping runs.
  • Game Management: Learning how to protect a lead or chase a game.

Training Info

Training Days:Mondays and Wednesdays

Time: 18:20 – 19:20

Location: Jimmy Bellows Sports Field

Focus: Confidence and fun on the ball.
