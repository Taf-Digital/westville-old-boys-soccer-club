GOALKEEPER ACADEMY

The Specialist Phase: Bravery & Precision

Development Focus

Goalkeepers require a unique psychological and physical toolkit. Our training isolates the specific demands of the position to build dominant shot-stoppers.

  • Shot Stopping: Mastering footwork, diving technique, and handling to neutralize goal threats.
  • Distribution: Developing the vision and range to act as the first point of attack through throws and kicks.
  • Command of the Box: Building the vocal leadership and bravery needed to own the penalty area.

Training Info

Location: Jimmy Bellows Sports Field

Focus: Confidence and fun on the ball.
