The Performance Phase: Tactical Discipline

Development Focus

Football becomes highly tactical. We prepare players for the intensity of senior football by focusing on system discipline and results.

  • Pressing Systems: How to win the ball back high up the pitch.
  • Unit Training: Specific work for defenders, midfielders, and strikers.
  • Mental Strength: Developing the focus needed for 80+ minute matches.

Training Info

Training Days:Mondays and Wednesdays

Time: 18:20 – 19:20

Location: Jimmy Bellows Sports Field

Focus: Confidence and fun on the ball.
