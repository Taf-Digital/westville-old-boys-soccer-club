UNDER 10 DIVISION

The Functional Phase: Pitch Awareness

Development Focus

A big transition year as we move to larger squads. We focus on "The Golden Age of Learning," where technical skills are absorbed rapidly.

  • First Touch: Receiving the ball effectively to set up the next move.
  • Positional Roles: Understanding the difference between defending and attacking roles.
  • Striking the Ball: Improving power and accuracy in shooting and long passing.

Training Info

Training Days:Mondays and Wednesdays

Time: 17:15 – 18:15

Location: Jimmy Bellows Sports Field

Focus: Confidence and fun on the ball.
