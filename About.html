ABOUT US

Discover the Heart of Our Team and Activities

Our Club

The club is one of the most established amateur football organizations in KwaZulu-Natal. While it has strong historical ties to Westville Boysâ€™ High School, it operates as a community club open to a wide range of players.


Our Emphasis

The club emphasizes "sports-for-all," focusing on teamwork, discipline, and community spirit. It serves as a bridge for school-leavers to continue their competitive or social football careers


About Our teams

The club typically fields several senior teams playing in the Durban Central and greater KZN leagues. They have a reputation for having a strong "social yet competitive" atmosphere


The Man of The Field

The fields are named after Jimmy Bellows, a local figure who was deeply instrumental in the development of community sports in Westville. He was a long-time administrator and a driving force behind the establishment of the sports club facilities that thousands of residents use today.


Also

Beyond football, "Jimmy Bellows" is well known in Durban for the Westville Night Market, which is held on the grounds
