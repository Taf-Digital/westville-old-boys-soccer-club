Coach Nicole

U6 Junior Coach

Coaching Mission & Philosophy

To grasp the fundamentals of soccer skills and techniques, understanding postional play and being part of team while experiencing joy and fun of it all.

I believe the best teams are the ones that have a good synergy with each other and their coach, so I want to foster that kind of synergy with the youth as well as foster a sense of love and fun of the sport as I was due to watching it over the years with my dad and watching my son Hudson play little league matches on sidelines.

Coaching Experience

  • Westville Old Boys FC - U6 Junior Coach (Current)
  • Coached teams and clubs in Cape Town and Durban

Grassroots Focus Areas

  • Touches-per-minute maximizing ball familiarity
  • Agility, balance, and spatial awareness mechanics
  • Confidence building & basic positional play
  • Specialized shot-stopping mechanics for youth goalkeepers
