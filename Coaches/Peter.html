Coach Peter

Youth Goalkeeper Coach & Foundation Phase Mentor

Coaching Mission & Philosophy

I believe that goalkeeping starts with joy, courage, and coordination. For our youngest athletes, the goal is to build a fearless mindset and master basic motor mechanics. By transforming core footwork and catching drills into engaging games, we set up a robust foundation for future shot-stoppers while keeping the pitch full of fun and energy.

Coaching History

  • Westville Old Boys FC — Goalkeeper Coach (Current)
  • Westville Old Boys FC — Former Under-6 Divisional Lead

Youth Specializations

  • Hand-eye coordination and spatial awareness games
  • Safe landing mechanics and basic diving posture
  • Footwork agility and lateral tracking
  • Confidence building and leadership at the back
