Coach Philani

U8 Junior Coach

Coaching Mission & Philosophy

My goal as a coach is to motivate and enable every athlete to realize their greatest potential on and off the field.

For the coming seasons we focus on developing a solid, encouraging team where hard work, effort, dedication and Respect are the cornerstones for everything we do.

As a team we continue to get better and improve everyday, learn from our mistakes and celebrate our successes.

I think its vital to create an environment where players feel supported, encouraged and challenged to reach their full potential both on the field and in life.

Coaching Experience

I have over 4 years of experience building and managing sucessful teams in various schools and clubs.

These experiences have allowed me to develop a deep understanding of player development and team dynamics further evolving my coaching methodolgies and knowledge of the game.
