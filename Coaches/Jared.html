Coach Jared Letord

Soccer Coach - Juniors

Coaching Mission

My mission as a soccer coach is to create a positive and enjoyable environment where both players and parents are happy. I strive to help young athletes reach their full potential, improve their skills, and develop a deeper understanding of the game.

Beyond just performance, I focus on how my players feel—building their confidence, resilience, and love for soccer.

Seeing my players grow and succeed, both on and off the field, is what drives me as a coach.

Coaching Experience

Currently in my third year of coaching, with two years of experience in developing and leading young soccer players. I have worked with various age groups, focusing on skill development, teamwork, and fostering a love for the game.

Coaching Philosophy

I believe in developing well-rounded athletes who excel both on and off the field. My approach to coaching is centred on skill development, teamwork, and fostering a growth mindset. I aim to create an environment where players feel encouraged to challenge themselves, embrace discipline, and enjoy the game.

By instilling values of respect, perseverance, and dedication, I help my players not only improve their soccer abilities but also grow as individuals.
